# Commercial Strategy

## Thesis

The public standard creates trust and adoption. Revenue comes from implementation, private modules, managed systems, audits, training, and specialized plugins.

## Offer Ladder

| Level | Offer | Buyer |
|---|---|---|
| Free | Public standard, templates, repo checklist. | Builders, creators, founders. |
| Starter | Module setup kit and examples. | Solo operators and small teams. |
| Sprint | Done-with-you implementation sprint. | Founders, creators, investors, agencies. |
| Pro | Private plugin or module pack. | Power users and teams. |
| Managed | Ongoing operating system support. | Businesses and portfolio operators. |
| Partner | White-label, licensing, training, and integration. | Agencies, funds, accelerators, enterprise teams. |

## First Sellable Offers

| Offer | Promise | Proof Needed |
|---|---|---|
| Domain Portfolio Sprint | Turn a domain list into buy, build, list, lease, hold, or pass decisions. | Sample report and validation. |
| Creator OS Launch Sprint | Turn one creator idea into assets, offer, launch kit, and distribution loop. | Example launch kit. |
| Business OS Founder Sprint | Turn scattered priorities into offer architecture, weekly command cadence, and metrics. | Founder command brief. |
| Investor OS Diligence Sprint | Turn a deal or thesis into a memo, risk register, diligence queue, and portfolio support plan. | Sample memo with disclaimers. |
| Agentic OS Audit | Score a repo or team against the standard and produce an upgrade path. | Compliance checklist. |

## Pricing Logic

Do not lead with broad platform pricing. Start with concrete service outcomes.

Suggested range:

- Starter kits: low-friction paid downloads.
- Sprints: project-priced based on scope.
- Pro modules: recurring access or annual license.
- Managed systems: monthly retainer.
- Partner/enterprise: custom license plus implementation.

## Safety Boundary

Do not market regulated outcomes. Investor OS is for organization, memo quality, diligence process, and portfolio support. Investment, legal, tax, accounting, and regulated decisions require qualified human review.
