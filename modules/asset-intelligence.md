# Agentic Asset Intelligence Module

## Definition

Agentic Asset Intelligence turns digital surfaces into verified, monetizable assets through agents, evidence gates, workflows, and operating ledgers.

Digital surfaces include domains, content, offers, workflows, agent systems, data rooms, landing pages, templates, social media, and business operations.

## First-Win Workflow

```text
surface -> asset thesis -> evidence queue -> monetization route -> proof artifact -> operating ledger
```

## Asset Classes

| Asset | Route |
|---|---|
| Domains | Buy, build, list, lease, hold, or pass. |
| Content | Publish, repurpose, bundle, or use as proof. |
| Offers | Lead magnet, starter kit, sprint, retainer, license. |
| Workflows | Template, playbook, plugin, managed system. |
| Agent Systems | Skill pack, plugin, MCP, runner adapter, pro module. |
| Business Ops | Dashboard, cadence, CRM, support, partner package. |

## Core Agents

| Agent | Output |
|---|---|
| Asset Strategist | Asset thesis and route. |
| Evidence Analyst | Verification queue. |
| Product Packager | Offer or product surface. |
| Distribution Lead | Channel and growth loop. |
| Risk Sentinel | Claims, privacy, legal, and approval gates. |
| Ledger Keeper | Evidence, owner, decision, and review date. |

## Proof Artifact

Asset intelligence packet with thesis, evidence, route, risk gate, next action, and ledger entry.
