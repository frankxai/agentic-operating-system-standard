# Agentic Investor OS Module

## Definition

Agentic Investor OS helps investors, founders, angels, funds, and accelerators organize thesis, deal flow, diligence, memos, portfolio support, and investor communications through explicit evidence and approval gates.

This module is for workflow, research organization, and operating discipline. It is not financial, legal, tax, accounting, or investment advice.

## Buyer

- Angel investor.
- Founder raising capital.
- Micro-fund.
- Accelerator.
- Portfolio operator.
- Startup studio.

## First-Win Workflow

```text
thesis -> opportunity intake -> diligence queue -> memo -> risk register -> decision gate -> portfolio support loop
```

## Core Agents

| Agent | Output |
|---|---|
| Thesis Architect | Investment or strategic thesis draft. |
| Deal Intake Analyst | Opportunity summary and missing information. |
| Diligence Lead | Evidence queue and risk register. |
| Market Analyst | Market notes with source requirements. |
| Memo Editor | IC memo or founder-facing diligence packet. |
| Portfolio Support Lead | 30-day support plan. |
| Compliance Sentinel | Blocks regulated claims and routes expert review. |

## Required Skills

- Research synthesis.
- Market mapping.
- Memo writing.
- Risk register design.
- Portfolio support.
- Data room organization.
- Compliance-aware communication.

## Approval Gates

- Investment decision gate.
- Legal review gate.
- Financial/tax/accounting gate.
- Confidential information gate.
- Public communication gate.
- Data room access gate.

## Proof Artifact

Sample diligence memo with thesis, evidence queue, risk register, open questions, and explicit human review requirements.
