# Agentic Creator OS Module

## Definition

Agentic Creator OS helps creators, studios, and founder-led brands turn ideas into publishable assets, offers, launches, and distribution loops through governed agents, skills, workflows, and safety gates.

## Buyer

- Creator.
- Founder.
- Studio.
- Agency.
- Educator.
- Media operator.

## First-Win Workflow

```text
idea -> brief -> asset plan -> production agents -> proof asset -> launch kit -> distribution loop
```

## Core Agents

| Agent | Output |
|---|---|
| Creative Director | Campaign thesis and asset map. |
| Content Strategist | Editorial plan and distribution logic. |
| Product Packager | Offer, download, template, or launch kit. |
| Visual Director | Visual language and asset QA. |
| Revenue Lead | Product ladder and checkout path. |
| Risk Sentinel | Claims, rights, attribution, and public/private review. |

## Required Skills

- Content strategy.
- Product packaging.
- Visual direction.
- Launch planning.
- Distribution.
- Rights and attribution.
- Analytics review.

## Approval Gates

- Public claim gate.
- Rights and attribution gate.
- Customer data gate.
- Paid offer gate.
- Brand use gate.

## Proof Artifact

Creator launch kit with one publishable asset, one offer page outline, one distribution plan, and one feedback loop.
