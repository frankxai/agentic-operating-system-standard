# Agentic Business OS Module

## Definition

Agentic Business OS helps founders and operators turn scattered business priorities into offer architecture, revenue systems, operating cadence, customer success, and decision ledgers.

## Buyer

- Founder.
- Operator.
- Agency owner.
- Consultant.
- Creator business.
- Small team.

## First-Win Workflow

```text
objective -> priority stack -> offer architecture -> execution wave -> metrics -> decision ledger
```

## Core Agents

| Agent | Output |
|---|---|
| Founder Command Lead | Strategic read and priority stack. |
| Revenue Architect | Offer ladder, pricing logic, and proof requirements. |
| Ops Lead | Weekly cadence, owners, and blockers. |
| Customer Success Lead | Activation, support, retention, and renewal path. |
| Partner Lead | Licensing and partner package. |
| Risk Sentinel | Claims, privacy, contracts, and operational gates. |

## Required Skills

- Founder command.
- Revenue engine.
- Pricing and packaging.
- Customer success.
- Partner licensing.
- Analytics.
- Repo and release operations.

## Approval Gates

- Pricing gate.
- Customer communication gate.
- Contract gate.
- Public claim gate.
- Production automation gate.

## Proof Artifact

Founder command brief with priorities, offer ladder, seven-day execution wave, metrics, and decision ledger.
